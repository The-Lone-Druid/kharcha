### Why Kharcha?

- **Simple & Intuitive**: No complex accounting knowledge required
- **Multi-Account Support**: Track all your financial accounts in one place
- **Real-time Sync**: Changes sync instantly across devices
- **Privacy-First**: Your data is yours, securely stored
- **Beautiful Design**: Modern UI that's pleasant to use daily

### Who is it for?

Kharcha is perfect for:

- Individuals wanting to track personal expenses
- Freelancers managing income and expenses
- Anyone wanting better visibility into their spending habits
- People who want a simple alternative to complex budgeting apps
